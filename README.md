## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Ferns N Petals sales data to understand customer buying patterns and business performance during different occasions and seasons.

The analysis includes:
- Revenue analysis across months and occasions
- Product-wise sales performance
- City-wise order analysis
- Customer spending behavior
- Order quantity analysis
- Day-wise revenue trends
- Interactive dashboard creation using Power BI
- Business reporting for decision-making

## Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to identify:
- Which occasions generate the highest revenue
- Which products perform best during festive seasons
- Which cities contribute the most orders
- Customer purchasing behavior and spending patterns
- High-performing sales days and seasonal trends
- Opportunities to improve sales strategy and customer targeting

This project helps solve these problems through analytical reporting and dashboard visualization.

- Key Insights
1. Revenue Performance
   
    - Total revenue generated was ₹ 35.2 Lakhs.
  
    - Revenue peaked during festival occasions like Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan.
  
    - Seasonal occasions significantly impacted customer purchasing behavior.
  
2. Product 
    - Magnam Set generated the highest revenue among all products.
  
    - Soft Toys and Sweets showed strong sales performance during festive periods.
  
    - Certain low-performing products contributed minimal revenue and may require promotional strategies.
  
3. Customer Analysis
   
    - Average customer spending was approximately ₹ 3,520.
  
    - Some customers placed significantly higher quantity orders, indicating bulk purchasing behavior.
  
    - Occasion-based shopping patterns strongly influenced spending.
  
4. Occasion Analysis
   
    - Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generated the highest sales revenue.
  
    - Diwali and Valentine’s Day showed moderate performance.
  
    - Occasion-specific campaigns can further increase seasonal sales.
  
5. Regional Analysis
   
    - Multiple cities contributed consistently to order volume.
  
    - Some regions generated higher order counts but lower overall revenue, indicating lower average order values.

## Recommendations

- Increase marketing campaigns during high-performing occasions like Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan.

- Focus on promoting top-performing products during festive seasons.
  
- Introduce targeted offers for low-performing occasions to improve sales.

- Improve inventory planning based on monthly demand trends.
  
- Use customer spending behavior to create personalized marketing strategies.
  
- Expand sales efforts in high-order cities to maximize revenue opportunities.
  
- Monitor product performance regularly to optimize product offerings.
